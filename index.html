About

I'm a Mechatronic Engineering student at UNSW with a deep focus on CAD, mechanical fabrication, and embedded systems. My work lives at the boundary between hardware and software. Where design decisions have physical consequences.

I've led mechanical design on vehicles that will soon compete internationally in China, run Cloudflare-secured home servers, and optimised 3D-printed assemblies to shave filament and time without sacrificing fit. I care about precision in tolerances, in code, and in how I explain things to others.

Outside engineering I teach HSC Physics, and I was honoured to receive the ADF Long Tan Future Innovators Award for excellence in STEM.
